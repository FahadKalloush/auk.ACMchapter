What is ACM?

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world’s largest professional organization for computing professionals and students. Our AUK Student Chapter connects students with global ACM resources, coding competitions, workshops, and real-world tech projects. Unlike regular university clubs, ACM gives you international recognition, exclusive access to certifications, networking with industry professionals, and hands-on experience that builds your future career in technology.

Our Chapter Advisor

Dr. Belal Gharaibeh

Associate Professor of Computer Engineering

Dr. Belal Gharaibeh is a passionate innovator and researcher with over 15 years of experience across academia and leading engineering industries. With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and LEAN certification from the University of Kentucky, he brings hands-on expertise in advanced materials and additive manufacturing. His projects often focus on sustainability and energy solutions for underserved communities, blending technology, creativity, and purpose. Dr. Gharaibeh continues to inspire students to think critically, build boldly, and use engineering to make a real impact.
